About Us

About Game

All of the cards are laid face down on the screen and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The player has to turn over pairs of matching cards. Click on the cards to flip them and try to remember the position of each card then you should match each two pairs by memory.

Our Story

In the vibrant tapestry of Nueva Ecija University of Science and Technology's College of Information and Communications Technology (CICT), we are not just individuals; we are a collective force of IT students bound by a shared passion for technology, innovation, and a commitment to excellence. As we unveil our Techcard, we invite you to explore the unique identity that defines us.

Values

Our Mission

Our mission is to craft a lively Emoji Memory Card Game that not only entertains but also challenges players' memory. We aspire to enhance cognitive skills, creating an enjoyable and accessible experience for individuals of all ages in the expressive world of emojis.

Our Plan

Our plan is to enhance your gaming adventure. We aim to introduce exciting new expansions, delve into the digital realm, build a vibrant community, and engage players globally. It's more than just a game; it's a comprehensive strategy for providing endless enjoyment and excitement.
